UniverCcity 151

Purpose: A faith-anchored, discipline-driven recovery program that blends spiritual connection, practical routines, personal growth, and media-inspired mindset shifts to sustain long-term sobriety while reconstructing your core.

Core Principles

  • Trust in God or the Universe as a guiding force.
  • Personal responsibility and accountability.
  • Daily sustainable practices.
  • Gratitude, resilience, and non-judgmental support.
  • Growth through consistency, small wins, and purposeful action.

The following outlines UniverCcity's six-month framework, providing a structured path of simple consistency leading to measurable growth and preparing you for the next six months.

Page 2

Journal Layout & Mirror Work

UniverCcity Journal Page Layout

Complete this every time you leave and return.

  • Date: Top left corner.
  • Time of Departure / Return
  • Purpose for Leaving: Rehab or self-improvement focus.
  • Personal Goal for Outing: Growth-minded intention.
  • Reflection: Describe what happened and lessons learned without judgment.

Follow this method to track progress, stay accountable, and grow your relationship with the Universe through UniverCcity. The hardest part is accepting how simple it is.

Mirror Work

  • Stand in front of a mirror and gaze into your eyes for at least three minutes.
  • Affirm your worth and acknowledge how you have grown.
  • Be proud of who you are and the changes you are making.
  • Practice self-love with discipline, time, commitment, patience, and forgiveness.
  • Express gratitude for who you are and the progress underway.

Page 3

Daily Vision & Structured Entries

After those three minutes, take two more minutes to envision the person you want to become. Picture yourself thriving and ready to help others. Let this vision fuel your determination. Do this daily.

The journey is about unlocking the potential within you and embracing who you are destined to become.

Elevated Log-In and Log-Out Journaling

  1. Begin each entry with "UniverCcity" in the top left corner plus the date. Write "151" on the top right to mark your commitment.
  2. Create a left margin framework:
    • Time: Leave two lined spaces.
    • Reason: Leave two lines.
    • Prayer: Leave three lines for guidance.
    • Goals: Leave three lines for intentions.
    • Achievements: Leave three lines to celebrate wins or lessons without shame.
