About My Book

The Wealthy Loser

The Wealthy Loser stems from the adversity I faced over four decades under the oppressive shadow of my stepmother, who sought to diminish me in her eyes. I narrate my journey of overcoming her abuse and reclaiming my identity.

At the tender age of 7, this tragically lost everything. The loss of my mother, and my dad’s new wife, my stepmother. My family’s history, once steeped in wealth, love and success, was turned upside down. As Germany and Russia invaded Poland during WWII, I thought the reality of their plight and their family’s generational trauma alongside my personal struggles. I paint a vivid picture of our lives, and the hopelessness within our family forever portrayed in my story.

In my book, I show my journey of self-healing in my one that will resonate deeply with anyone who wants to make a difference in their own life and your world. I envision the involvement of esteemed Steven Spielberg in bringing my story to life. Now I am healed and learning to find the full extent of my narratives yet to be revealed. Reading “The Wealthy Loser,” you will uncover layers beyond what meets the eye and witness the resilience and greatness that emerges.
